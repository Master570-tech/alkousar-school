About AL KOUSAR SCHOOL

AL KOUSAR SCHOOL is a forward-thinking educational institution dedicated to excellence in academics, moral values, and community development. Our school serves students from Pre-K to Grade 12, offering a nurturing environment where every learner is encouraged to thrive intellectually, socially, and spiritually.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide holistic education that nurtures the mind and heart, instilling knowledge, ethics, and leadership in our students.

Vision: To be a leading school known for academic excellence, character development, and community service, guided by values of integrity, compassion, and respect.
